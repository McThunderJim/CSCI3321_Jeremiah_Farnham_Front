Chocolate Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  • Beat the butter, eggs, sugar, and vanilla until light and fluffy
  • Add flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt
  • Mix ingredients together
  • Add Chocolate chips
  • Scoop dough on to cookie pans
  • Bake 8-10 minutes
  • Let cookies cool

Alfredo Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Directions

  • Melt butter over medium to low heat
  • Add cream and simmer for 5 minutes
  • Add garlic and cheese
  • Whisk quickly
  • Stir in parsley
  • Ready to serve

Oreo Cookie Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • 36 OREO Cookies, finely crushed
  • 16 ounces of melted semi-sweet baking chocolate

Directions

  • Mix cookie crumbs and creeme chees
  • Shape into 1-inch balls
  • Freeze for 10 minutes
  • Dip into melted chocolate
  • Place in waxed paper-line pan
  • Refrigerate for one hour
